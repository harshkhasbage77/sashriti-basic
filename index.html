The Problem

E-waste is a gold mine we keep burning.

Every tonne of electronics contains valuable materials, yet most end up in landfills or incinerated.

  • Toxic emissions
  • Low recovery rates
  • Energy-intensive & hazardous
  • Unviable at scale in India

Our Solution

Our proprietary Cryogenic Pulverization process cleanly separates valuable materials from waste PCBs using a unique combination of extreme cold, mechanical precision, and smart separation technologies.

No acids. No incinerators. No environmental side-effects.

Output stream:

  1. High-purity copper
  2. Base metal concentrates
  3. Gold-enriched fractions
  4. Recovered resin powders

Why It’s Different: Traditional recycling methods are messy, dangerous, and downright primitive.

Our approach is dry, scalable, and clean, setting a new standard for efficiency and environmental responsibility — all without the toxic footprint.
